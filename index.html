Dynamic Light's patented technology uses the principles of dynamic light scattering to generate ‘speckle’ images which have unique characteristics. Speckle imaging enables non-invasive real-time, continuous, and quantitative visualization of blood flow in living tissues. This capability is of great current interest for health care since it enables monitoring of tissue perfusion during surgery.
